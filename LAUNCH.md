## Short post

I released `research-paper-lifecycle-skills`: 42 reusable AI agent skills for the work around academic papers.

It covers literature discovery, writing, citation verification, CFP/venue checks, submission preflight, anonymization, rebuttal planning, camera-ready prep, artifacts, slides, talk scripts, Q&A rehearsal, and posters.

No bundled papers or copyrighted corpora. The skills are public, inspectable, and designed to re-check live sources where rules matter.
